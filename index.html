school achievements

Field DAY
Field DAY is lots of FUN for everyone

The day before Field Day teachers assign you with other students in different grades and they randomize it but the 8th graders don't do Field Day because they organize Field Day but its fair because they get 1 week extra of Summer Break then everyone else does. So the teachers tell you to wear a color it can either be on your shirt, pant, shoes, or even bandana and then you get to play really fun games for half of the day like tug of war, dodgeball, capture the flag, and many more and everyone enjoys it because its so much fun.
